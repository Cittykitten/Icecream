Our Story

Scoops & Dreams began in 2010 with a simple mission: to create the most delicious, creamy ice cream using locally sourced ingredients and traditional methods.

Today, we still make our ice cream in small batches, ensuring every scoop is perfect. We're passionate about creating unique flavors that bring joy to our customers.
